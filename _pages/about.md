Hi, I’m **Anthony Truong**, a software engineer with experience in building backend and full-stack applications. My background is in **Computer Science**, and I recently graduated with **Master’s degree in Information Systems at Stevens Institute of Technology** to further strengthen my skills in software engineering, systems design, and data-driven technologies.

I specialize in building scalable applications using **Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, SQL, REST APIs, and microservices**, with additional experience in frontend development using **Angular, React, TypeScript, and JavaScript**. Across my academic and professional projects, I have worked on enterprise applications, backend services, web platforms, and system integrations, always with a strong focus on clean architecture, maintainability, and performance.

Before coming to the United States, I worked as a software engineer in France, contributing to projects involving business applications, UI improvements, software modernization, and technical documentation. More recently, I have continued expanding my experience through projects involving **Spring Boot applications, full-stack development, cloud-oriented architectures, and software design best practices**.

In addition to software engineering, I am also interested in **data analytics, machine learning, and system design**. I enjoy exploring how technology can solve practical business problems and improve user experiences. I am especially motivated by opportunities where I can keep learning, contribute to impactful products, and work with strong engineering teams.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about **gaming, technology**. I enjoy learning new tools, preparing for technical interviews, building side projects, and staying up to date with modern software engineering practices.
